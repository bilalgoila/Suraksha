UpSuraksha: Your Safety, Our Priority

A smart, secure, and simple app that connects citizens with local police for instant help, complaint filing, tracking, and feedback.

Key Features for Citizens

Instant SOS Alert

With one tap, alert police, auto-share your live location (for 15 minutes), and get rapid assistance—even in severe emergencies.

Easy Complaint Filing

File complaints (harassment, fire, etc.) via text, audio, or photos, with an option for anonymity and automatic location tagging for accurate routing.

Nearby Police Support

Find the closest police stations quickly and easily using a dynamic map or list, ensuring immediate offline assistance if needed.

Real-Time Location Sharing

Share your live route and whereabouts with police for extra peace of mind when you feel unsafe or need escort support.

Complaint Status & Feedback

View all complaints and their live status, get notifications, and provide ratings/feedback to help improve safety services.

Safe & Secure

Misuse prevention, fake report blocking, complaint limits, and the ability to report police misconduct—all actions are logged for accountability.

How It Works

  1. Quick Onboarding: Register easily with your phone number, select language, accept T&Cs, and enable location.
  2. One-Tap SOS: In emergencies, confirm and instantly notify nearest police with your live location and incident details.
  3. Smart Complaint Routing: Reports are sent directly to the right SHO and nearest constable for speedy action.
  4. Live Status: Track every complaint from 'Received' to 'Closed'. Get notified for all updates.
  5. Feedback & Ratings: After closure, rate police response and submit suggestions for better service quality.

Analytics & Safety

Trends & Performance

Admins track complaints by area, incident type, and officer, with response times and public satisfaction metrics.

Misuse Prevention

Users are limited to 3 complaints per day. Fake reports can be blocked/flagged instantly.

Accountability

Citizens can report police misuse, every action is time-stamped for fair review.

Audit Trails

Detailed logging for every complaint, change, and user/officer action to ensure complete transparency.
