UpSuraksha: Your Safety, Our Priority

A smart, secure, and simple app that connects citizens with local police for instant help, complaint filing, tracking, and feedback.

Key Features for Citizens

Instant Missing Child Alert

Quickly broadcast missing child alerts to all nearby users, mobilizing the community for rapid recovery and safety.

Safe House Network

Find the nearest police chowki, thana, or check post. In emergencies, send pre-alerts with your location, media, and timestamp.

Area-wise Criminal Caution List

Stay informed about local threats like fraudsters and chain snatchers. Quietly upload photo/video proof for swift police action.

Smart Complaint Filing

Report incidents with descriptions and media, choose anonymity, and automatically tag your location for rapid police routing.

Live Panic Mode

Activate instant SOS and share your live location with police for 15 minutes. Use "Track Me" for real-time safety monitoring in emergencies.

Complaint Tracking & Feedback

Track complaints with real-time status, review your history, and provide feedback or ratings to improve police service quality.

Misuse Prevention & Accountability

Complaint limits prevent spam; all actions are logged with timestamps. Citizens can report police misconduct for transparency.

Quick & Secure Onboarding

Sign up using OTP, select your language, accept privacy terms, and enable live location for tailored and secure citizen experience.

How It Works

  1. Quick Onboarding: Register easily with your phone number, select language, accept T&Cs, and enable location.
  2. One-Tap SOS: In emergencies, confirm and instantly notify nearest police with your live location and incident details.
  3. Smart Complaint Routing: Reports are sent directly to the right SHO and nearest constable for speedy action.
  4. Live Status: Track every complaint from 'Received' to 'Closed'. Get notified for all updates.
  5. Feedback & Ratings: After closure, rate police response and submit suggestions for better service quality.
